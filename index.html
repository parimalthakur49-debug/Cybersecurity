What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity means protecting computers, networks and data from cyber attacks, hackers and unauthorized access.

Common Cyber Threats

🎣 Phishing

Fake emails or websites used to steal information.

🦠 Malware

Malicious software that can damage systems.

🔐 Password Attacks

Hackers try to guess or steal passwords.

📞 Fraud Calls

Scammers use fake phone calls to collect personal details, bank information or sensitive data.

📩 Fraud SMS

Fake SMS messages may contain harmful links designed to steal personal information or access accounts.

🔑 OTP Scam

Scammers may ask for OTPs to access accounts. Never share your OTP with anyone.

🏦 Banking Fraud

Banking fraud happens when attackers use fake calls, messages or websites to steal financial information.
